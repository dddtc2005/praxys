How should zone integration look on the Training page?

Two approaches for integrating zone ranges, dynamic distribution, and target comparison

A

Integrated Enhancement

Enhance the existing Training Diagnosis card — replace the hardcoded 4-zone bar with a dynamic bar that adapts to the selected theory's zones. Add a "target vs actual" comparison row below the bar, and show zone ranges in a compact table within the same card.

B

Separate Zone Card

Keep the existing diagnosis card focused on metrics + distribution bar. Add a new "Training Zones" card below it that shows zone ranges, target vs actual comparison chart, and diagnostic text. The distribution bar in the diagnosis card still adapts to the theory.
