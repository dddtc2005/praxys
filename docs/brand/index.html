I / vi

The name

Praxys is a variant of the Greek praxis — action grounded in theory. We keep the meaning and the sound, sharpen the trademark path with a single-letter shift. Not training prescribed from above, not science abstracted from the athlete, but method practiced by a person in their own life.

Short form Praxys is the hero mark — used in the sidebar, favicon, and every in-product surface. Full form Praxys Endurance carries category signal where discovery depends on it — App Store, marketing headers, the first sentence of a cold introduction.

II / vi

The flag

A race flag on a pole — the filled green flag reads as action and signal, the cobalt pole reads as grounded method. The fluttering trailing edge carries motion: not prescribed, not static. Together, the mark still holds the silhouette of a capital P — pole as stem, flag as bowl.

★ Selected

Praxys flag

Green race flag on a cobalt pole. The trailing edge pinches inward — a flag caught in the wind, reading as motion. Single visual idea; scales from 16 px favicon to hero marketing without loss.

Construction

On a 48 × 48 grid

The pole stands 37 units tall with a subtle 2-unit forward lean. The flag occupies the top quarter — 16 units tall, 24 units wide — with a single inward curve on the trailing edge at the vertical mid-point.

Clear space

One flag width

Minimum clear space on all sides equals the flag's width. Never crop the pole below 75 %, never tint the fill, never invert the stem/flag color roles.

III / vi

Green & cobalt

Green carries action — the training signal, the go, positive deltas. Cobalt is reserved for reasoning surfaces: why this recommendation, which theory, which source. The palette separates what to do from why the system suggested it.

VI / vi

One locale per surface

Every product surface — landing page, app chrome, marketing page, email, social card — renders in exactly one language. A Chinese reader sees Chinese. An English reader sees English. Never mix them on the same page. Parallel language pairings belong only in brand-study artifacts like this document; they are never functional copy.
